## 🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing supply chain data to uncover insights related to delivery performance, sales, profitability, and operational risks. Using SQL for data analysis and Power BI for visualization, the project helps in identifying inefficiencies and improving business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze order and delivery performance  
- Identify shipping delays and logistics inefficiencies  
- Evaluate sales, revenue, and profit trends  
- Understand customer segments and product performance  
- Assess the impact of discounts and delays on profitability  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **SQL (MySQL)** – Data querying and analysis  
- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard creation  
- **Excel/CSV** – Data source  

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset contains supply chain order-level data including:
- Order details (order_id, order_date)  
- Customer information (name, segment, location)  
- Product details (category, product name)  
- Shipping details (mode, delay, delivery status)  
- Financial metrics (sales, profit, discount)  

---

## 🔍 Key Business Questions

### 🧹 Data Cleaning & Validation
- Total records, unique orders, and duplicate checks  

### 🚚 Delivery Performance
- What is the average shipping delay?  
- Which shipping mode has the highest delay?  
- What percentage of orders are late, on-time, or early?  
- Which regions experience the most delays?  

### 💰 Sales & Revenue Analysis
- What is the total sales generated?  
- Which categories contribute the most revenue?  
- How does revenue vary by customer segment?  
- Which cities and countries generate the highest sales?  

### 👥 Customer Insights
- Who are the top customers by sales?  
- What is the order distribution across customer segments?  

### 📦 Product Analysis
- Which products are best-selling and least-selling?  
- Which products are most profitable or loss-making?  

### ⚠️ Risk & Operational Analysis
- Which regions have the highest late delivery %?  
- What is the average delay across regions?  
- How do discounts impact delivery performance and profit?  

---

## 📊 Power BI Dashboard

The dashboard includes:
- 📌 KPI Overview (Total Orders, Revenue, Late Delivery %)  
- 🚚 Shipping Performance Analysis  
- 🌍 Regional Risk Analysis  
- 📉 Profitability & Discount Insights  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Over **50% of orders are delivered late**, indicating operational inefficiencies  
- **Standard Class shipping** shows the highest delay rate  
- Certain regions contribute significantly to **revenue at risk**  
- Higher discount levels may increase delivery delays and reduce profitability  
- Some products generate high sales but lower profit margins  

---

## 💼 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how SQL and Power BI can be used together to transform raw supply chain data into actionable business insights. It highlights key problem areas and supports data-driven decision-making.
